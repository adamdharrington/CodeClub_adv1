The Batman cap

The Batman cap is my favorite hat. It has the Batman logo on the front.

It has the retro style logo from the 1980's TV shows starring Adam West who played Batman and Burt Ward who played Robin.

Why I like it

I like this hat because it reminds me of my favorite superhero, Batman. I also like the blue and yellow colors of the logo.

I purchased my hat and my daughter's matching hat from a local comic book store and I love when we wear them together.

My Brown beanie hat

This hat was a gift from my wife. It is a simple brown beanie that keeps me warm during the winter months.

Why I like it

I like this hat because it is very comfortable and it fits well. The brown color goes with many of my outfits.

Trivia

In Canada beanies are often called toques (pronounced "toohk"). A toque is a knitted hat that is typically worn in cold weather to keep the head warm.
