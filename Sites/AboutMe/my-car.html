Nissan Pulsar | 2015

My car is a 2015 Nissan Pulsar. It is a compact hatchback that is fuel efficient and reliable.

Why I like it

I like my car because it is easy to drive and park. It felt very new when I bought it used in 2020 and it has low kilometers. The fuel efficiency is fantastic even when petrol prices are high.

Features

Bluetooth

The car has Bluetooth connectivity so I can connect my phone and play music or make hands-free calls.

Backup camera

The car has a backup camera that helps me see behind me when reversing.

Air conditioning

The car has air conditioning that keeps me cool during hot days.

Automatic transmission

The car has an automatic transmission that makes it easy to drive in traffic and that was a must for my wife whose licence restricts her to autos.

Massive boot

The car has a large boot space that can fit all my groceries and buggy and I've transported tons of large items in it with ease.
