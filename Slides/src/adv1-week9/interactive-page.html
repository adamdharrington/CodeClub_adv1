Exercise 4: Hover Effects

Bonus Challenge

Instructions:

Make a box that changes color when you hover over it!

  • When mouse enters: background turns yellow
  • When mouse leaves: background turns back to lightblue

Your HTML:

    Hover over me!
</div>

Your JavaScript (template to complete):

let box = document.getElementById('_____');

box.onmouseenter = function() {
    box.style.backgroundColor = '____';
};
